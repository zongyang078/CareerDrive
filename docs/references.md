# Data Sources & References

## Primary Data

- **O\*NET Web Services API**: https://services.onetcenter.org/
  - Occupation details, skills, knowledge, abilities, education requirements, wage data
  - Free academic access with registration

- **Career_Drive_Project_Data_Sources.xlsx** (provided by client)
  - Construction O\*NET Codes: 30 occupation codes across building, highway, and utility sectors
  - JobBoards - DOT Prequal List: 60 pre-qualified contractors with websites
  - AGC Member List: 228 companies (General Contractors, Specialty, Suppliers, Service Providers, Developers)
  - AGC Sponsored Apprenticeships: 21 apprenticeship programs with training hours
  - Community College Programs: 25 programs across Maine community colleges
  - UMaine Programs: 12 engineering/construction programs in UMaine system
